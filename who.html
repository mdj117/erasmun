Gustave

Secretary General

Born and raised in Brussels, Gustave grew up in a French/Flemish environment. His hunger for knowledge and his multidisciplinary interests inspired him to major in Politics, Philosophy and Economics at EUC. He first encountered MUN in high school and attended various conferences since then. He co-organized the very first EUCMUN and works now on further making it a high-quality conference. Next to MUN, Gustave is organizing activities and summer camps for a scouting movement in Brussels. If he is not dreaming about skying, you will find him enjoying a {Dutch/good} beer. He is looking forward to welcome you in (online) Rotterdam and excited to share with you the ERASMUN experience!
