ErasMUN
April 10th - 11th 2021
Apply Now!

ErasMUN is an annual Model United Nations conference hosted by the MUN Committee, at the Erasmus University College in Rotterdam. In our unforgettable conferences you will get to simulate a United Nations debate, by representing your allocated state in the given body of the United Nations. Most importantly, however, you will also get to meet like-minded people and make new friendships! The members of the MUNco are constantly working on bettering the conference, learning from mistakes and building on successes. We are a group ambitious and motivated university students that want to merge academics with leisure, and enjoy creating exceptional opportunities for others, such us our MUN conference. Our team is divided into an executive team and an MUN committee. We offer regular delegation trainings to maintain a high level of professionalism. Furthermore, we collaborate with other committees to extend our understanding of a wide range of topics, ranging from politics to sustainability.


Can't wait to host you on the 10th of April!
