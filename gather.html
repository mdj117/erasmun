Esteemed Delegates, Chairs, and Friendly Visitors,


On behalf of the whole team, I am delighted to welcome you to ERASMUN 2021. The conference will take place on the 10th of April and include 100 delegates in an online environment. ERASMUN brings together youth to understand, debate and tackle today’s challenges. The conference’s topic is Sustainability - ensuring the future ecologically, socially and economically. The covid-19 crisis can be an opportunity to rebound and rethink the world of tomorrow. Our amazing team, composed of ultra-motivated members with diverse talents, was able to design an original and high-quality conference. The one-day format enables to limit screen time while ensuring a dynamic debate. The online platform (Gather Town) literally enables you to walk around, connect and interact almost like in real life. Moreover, quality guest speakers will be invited to stimulate the debate and enable you to deepen your knowledge. By joining us, you will learn about and help finding solutions to global challenges, develop skills and foremost meet amazing people! Last year, we welcomed participants originating from 40 different countries, and we expect more this year with the online format. I am looking forward to welcome you in (online) Rotterdam and excited to share with you the ERASMUN experience!


See you on the 10th of April,

Gustave Mondron

ErasMUN 2021 Secretary General

Rotterdam
Commercial Capital of the Netherland

We are located in the world-famous city of Rotterdam, home to the great thinker Erasmus! Our city is well known for its great port, intense in- and exports, modern architecture and busyness! Rotterdam has completely been bombed to the grounds during an attack in WWII. However, new times brought new things to look up to, and the city has been rebuilt with marvellous architectural designs, such as the Erasmus Bridge, crossing over the Maas! Rotterdam has many lively places to explore, ranging from big busy streets to small hidden parks and sunny docks in the port!

Come and join our conference to experience Rotterdam first hand, make great connections and debate over something you are passionate about!
